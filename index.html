The 3 Pillars of Siddha

"According to Siddha, a healthy body is the result of a balanced proportion of the three vital forces (Tridoshas)."

Vatham

Air & Space

Governs movement, nervous system, and respiration. Imbalance leads to pain, dryness, and anxiety.

Pitham

Fire

Governs digestion, metabolism, and intellect. Imbalance causes acidity, heat, and inflammation.

Kapham

Earth & Water

Governs structure, strength, and lubrication. Imbalance leads to cold, congestion, and lethargy.
